Home page

About Me

Allow me to introduce myself. My name is Nicholas Wilkins and I am the creator and administrator of this website. I was born and raised in South Jordan, Utah as the 3rd of 8 children. I married the love of my life in the summer of 2021 and we are expecting our first child later this spring. I have worked in the financial industry for the last four years, but I am currently working on transitioning my skillset to a new industry - software engineering. I currently have experience in web development with HTML/CSS, database design and interactions with MySQL, and programming in Python.

Goal of This Website

The goal of this website is to build a portfolio website that showcases my skills and personality, and that attracts the attention of potential employers and clients. I want to demonstrate my proficiency and attention to detail in HTML/CSS, as well as other languages that I will learn along the way. I also want to use this website to display my flexibility and creativity with design. This website will be updated and expanded as needed while I continue to grow, learn, and develop skills within the industry and at school. For more information on my education and projects, please see their respective pages via the navigation bar.

Projects

CSE 110 - Introduction to Programming

This course is the first course taken by Software Engineering students here at Brigham Young University - Idaho. The purpose of this course is to teach students the fundamentals of programming using the interpretive and easy to learn language known as Python. Each week, the coursework and projects get progressively more difficult. The one that gave me the most trouble out of all of them was one that is based on the game Wordle, and I have included an image of the running and completed program below. The hardest part was combining my knowledge of lists and comparing inputs to the winning word from the list. Once that was figured out, the rest was easy!

WDD 130 - Web Fundamentals

This course is also taken by every new Software Engineering Student at Brigham Young University - Idaho. This course covers the basics of website design and development using HTML and CSS. The initial learning of HTML/CSS is extremely difficult, but luckily there is plenty of opportunity to overcome humps in the course. Throughout the semester there are two large projects undertaken - a guided one to build a white water rafting website and one where we build our own personal website, which is the website you are now looking at. Below, I have included an image of the website I created for the guided project.

Educational Background

I am currently a Software Engineering (B.S.) student at Brigham Young University - Idaho in Rexburg, Idaho. My current emphasis is in two different types of engineering, Software Design and Full Stack Web Development. I currently am in my junior year of University and have a 4.0 GPA as of the time that this is being written for the website. Based on my current rate of progress and the remaining coursework in front of me, it is estimated that I will be graduating with my degree in July 2026. Prior to my time here at BYU-I, received an Associate Degree with High Honors in University Studies from Utah Valley University in May 2023, where I also had a 4.0 GPA.

Accomplishments and Achievements

As a very hard worker with a strong work ethic, I have my fair share of accomplishments, although some are definitely more impressive than others. Most recently, I earned a full tuition scholarship from BYU-I and have been able to maintain it due to my grades. As I earn more accolades, I will be sure to note them here.
